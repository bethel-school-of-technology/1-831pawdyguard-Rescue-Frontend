Hi, my name is Pitter! My first blog...
I was asked to share a bit of my story. To be honest, it is the first time somebody asked me to write about my life. I feel honored and I'm so excited!

Let's start with the simple stuff to warm up. I live in Salinas, CA with my lovely homy Nia Casad. And it's not too far fetched to say, I owe her my life.
Nia's mum was rescuing pets back in the day, so she grew up with a big heart for animals, a good example and she followed in the footsteps of her mum. At 10 Nia started volunteering at a vet, learning animal care from scratch, was hired with 16, and stepped out to do her own rescue with 19.

We met through a TNR program. I was trapped as a little kitten. My lucky day, I tell you. I would have died without it. Nia was called and she took me in. For the next three weeks she kept me at her side, fed me, bought medication, made sure I would survive. And I did. And because I'm so incredibly cute and charming, she decided that I could live with her. What shall I say, she couldn't but love me! And I know, I'm 'just' a cat, but her animal rescue made a huge difference, for me and hundreds of others.

And what I see - it even makes a difference for the people who donate and volunteer. It brings the best out of them. Thank you for your support- the time and the money you invest!

Yours sincerely, Pitter
